About

Mindful Moments Tracker is my INF601 Final Project. I built it as a simple place to pause, check in, and notice how each day feels. Users can write short notes, choose a mood, and receive a supportive quote through the ZenQuotes API.

This project builds on what I learned earlier in the semester with Flask, templates, forms, databases, and API integration.
